Stranger Things (Season 1)

Chapter One: The Shadow Beneath Hawkins

In the quiet town of Hawkins, a young boy named Will Byers mysteriously disappears one night. As his friends search for him, they meet a strange girl with a shaved head who calls herself Eleven. She has powerful abilities and seems to know something about Will’s disappearance. At the same time, Will’s mother Joyce believes her son is trying to communicate with her through flickering lights, while the town’s police chief Jim Hopper uncovers dark secrets hidden beneath Hawkins Laboratory. The lab has been experimenting with dangerous forces that opened a doorway to a terrifying parallel world known as the Upside Down. As the friends work together, they discover that a monstrous creature from this other world is hunting people in Hawkins. Friendship, courage, and sacrifice become the only way to fight the unknown and bring Will back.

Stranger Things (Season 2)

Chapter Two: Hawkins Under the Shadow

Nearly a year after Will Byers’ return, life in Hawkins appears to be returning to normal. However, the Upside Down has not let go so easily. Will begins experiencing terrifying visions of a massive shadow creature watching over the town. These visions are signs that something dark is slowly spreading beneath Hawkins. Meanwhile, Eleven struggles to find her identity and learns the truth about her past and her powers. As strange vines begin to infect the town and people fall under the control of the Upside Down, it becomes clear that a powerful entity known as the Mind Flayer is trying to take over the real world. The friends must once again rely on trust, bravery, and sacrifice to protect Hawkins. As the darkness grows stronger, they realize that escaping the Upside Down once was not the end — it was only the beginning.

Stranger Things (Season 3)

Chapter Three: The Summer When Hawkins Broke

During the summer of 1985, Hawkins seems lively and happy with the opening of the Starcourt Mall. But beneath the bright lights and music, a new danger is growing. The Mind Flayer returns in a terrifying new form, taking control of people and building a monster from their bodies. As friendships are tested and secrets unfold, the group discovers that a hidden Russian laboratory beneath the mall is reopening the gate to the Upside Down. The battle becomes more intense and personal, leading to sacrifice and loss. By the end of the summer, Hawkins is changed forever, and the group realizes that growing up can be just as painful as fighting monsters.

Stranger Things (Season 4)

Chapter Four: The Curse of the Upside Down

Months after the Starcourt tragedy, the group is separated and struggling with grief and guilt. A new and terrifying villain named Vecna emerges, killing victims by targeting their deepest fears and emotional pain. His attacks reveal that the Upside Down is more connected to Hawkins than ever before. As the friends race across different locations to stop Vecna, they uncover shocking truths about Eleven’s past and the origin of the Upside Down. The battle against Vecna pushes everyone to their limits, and despite their efforts, Hawkins suffers devastating damage. The line between the real world and the Upside Down begins to collapse.

Stranger Things (Season 5)

Chapter Five: The Final Battle for Hawkins

With the Upside Down fully invading Hawkins, the final season focuses on one last fight to save the world. Vecna’s plan is revealed as the ultimate threat, and the fate of Hawkins — and humanity — hangs in the balance. Old friendships are tested, past sacrifices gain meaning, and everyone must face their fears one last time. As the group comes together for a final stand, they learn that courage, love, and unity are stronger than any darkness. The story concludes with the end of the Upside Down’s control and a bittersweet farewell to Hawkins, marking the end of an unforgettable journey.

About This Project

This project is a creative web design inspired by the Netflix series Stranger Things. The main objective of this project is to present the story, characters, and themes of the series using only HTML and CSS. Special focus has been given to visual effects such as dark themes, animations, and the Upside Down portal effect, which are created without using JavaScript. This project shows how CSS can be used not only for styling but also for creating interactive and cinematic experiences. Through this project, I learned about layout design, CSS animations, pseudo-elements, and creative problem-solving in web development.
